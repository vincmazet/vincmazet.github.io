Research

ICube (UMR 7357) / team IMAGeS

My research activities focus on inverse problems in signal & image processing. I generally use Bayesian approaches, MCMC algorithms, and sparse approximation methods.

  • Improving measures of a sensors network on electrical installations (with Socomec, Hamza Nait Hmid's Ph.D., in progress)
  • Fast detection of unicellular microorganisms by high-resolution imagery (with Redberry, Iris Daurensan's Ph.D., 2021
  • Joint Bayesian decomposition of spectroscopic signal sequences (Hassan Mortada's Ph.D., 2018)
  • Detection of faint extended sources in astronomy (Jean-Baptiste Courbot's PhD, 2017)
  • Building detection in remote sensing (Tran Thanh Ngo's Ph.D., 2015)
  • Spectro-morphological galaxy classification (Benjamin Perret's Ph.D., 2010)
  • Decomposition of spectroscopic signals into peaks
  • Simulation of truncated Gaussian distributions
  • Baseline correction in spectroscopy

Research projects :

  • DAHLIA (ANR BLANC, 2009-2012)
